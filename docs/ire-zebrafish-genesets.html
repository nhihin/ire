4. Defining gene sets

  • The gene set definitions are:

    • 3’UTR HQ: Genes containing high-quality IREs in the 3’ UTR

    • 3’UTR ALL: Genes containing any predicted IREs in the 3’ UTR (low, med or high quality)

    • 5’UTR HQ: Genes containing high-quality IREs in the 5’ UTR

    • 5’UTR ALL: Genes containing any predicted IREs in the 5’ UTR (low, med or high quality)

  • The genes containing both 3’ and 5’ IREs will be removed from these gene sets.
